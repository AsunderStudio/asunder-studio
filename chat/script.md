## Notes for editing

- **Adding/removing lines** is fine — I'll re-parse the whole file.
- **Renaming characters** — keep `Frances` and `Joseph` spelled exactly, or tell me the new names.
- **Timestamps in PART 1** drive nothing functional; they're just shown on the bubble. Edit freely.
- **Emoji reactions**: append `{emoji: 🔥}` to a line. Only one per line.
- **CTA**: exactly one `{cta}` line per SET, and it should be the last meaningful beat. (Other lines can follow but the email button won't re-appear.)
- **Delays** in PART 2 are the pause *before* the typing indicator for that line starts. Total time between bubbles also includes the typing animation.
